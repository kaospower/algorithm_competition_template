transmission rate of a link(链路的传输速率)  
bits/second(比特/秒),bit/s,bps  

packets(分组,数据包):When one end system has data to send to another end system,
the sending end system segments the data and adds header bytes to each segment.  

communication links(通信链路):made up of different types of physical media,including coaxial cable(同轴电缆),  
copper wire(铜线),optical fiber(光纤) and radio spectrum(无线电频谱).  
packet switch(分组交换机):takes a packet arriving on one of its incoming communication
links(入通信链路) and forwards(转发) that packet on one of its outgoing communication links(出通信链路).   

Link-layer switches are typically used in access networks(接入网),while routers are typically
used in the network core(网络核心).  

route/path through the network(通过该网络的路径):The sequence of communication links and
packet switches traversed by a packet from the sending end system to the receiving end system.
